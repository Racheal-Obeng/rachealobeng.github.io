Public Understanding of Big Data

Conducted community outreach to educate schools, health centers, and local communities on the role of big data in healthcare. Emphasized data ethics, privacy, and the potential benefits of sharing de-identified health data for disease surveillance and treatment.

Engaged with diverse groups to gauge perceptions on data usage and identify key concerns. Findings highlighted the importance of transparent communication and policy frameworks to build public trust in healthcare data initiatives.
